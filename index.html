Singapore Demonlist Guidelines

Adapted from pointercrate.com. This list tracks Singapore's best Geometry Dash players.

1. Level Eligibility

Only demons on the official pointercrate Top 150 are eligible. Levels are added within 7 days of pointercrate placement. Legacy levels (formerly top 150) give 0 points but remain archived.

2. Record Requirements

  • Minimum %: Top 5: 100% | 6-20: 75% | 21-75: 70% | 76-150: 60%
  • Must be achieved legitimately on 60Hz minimum. 144Hz+ recommended for top 30.
  • No secret routes, no noclip, no speedhack, no macros, no physics bypass.

3. Video Proof

Full attempt with audio required. Must show: level, end stats, previous death or attempts list for credibility. For Top 10 completions, raw footage or handcam may be requested. Cut videos are rejected.

4. Verification & Timing

Records are dated by Singapore Time (UTC+8). Date is video upload or completion date, whichever is earlier. Mods may request additional proof for suspected illegitimate runs.

5. Nationality

Only Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents currently residing in Singapore qualify for the national list and stats. Submit with NRIC/FIN verification to mods (DM) for first record.

6. Points System (2025)

We use pointercrate formula: #1 = 350.00 pts. Formula: points = 350 / (1 + e^((rank-1)/30)) approximated. Full completion awards full points.

Partial runs (below 100% but above minimum) award: points × (percent/100)^5 × 0.1. This heavily rewards completions.

Player score = sum of all their best record points.

7. Submission Process

Use the Submit page. Mods review within 48 hours. Rejected records will include reason. Ban evasion or cheating results in permanent blacklist from SG Demonlist.
